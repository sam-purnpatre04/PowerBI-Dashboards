# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes employee attrition, workforce demographics, and job satisfaction.

The objective is to identify factors affecting employee retention and help HR make data-driven decisions.

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- DAX
- Power Query
- Excel

---

## Dataset

HR_Data.xlsx

---

## Dashboard Features

- Employee Count
- Attrition Count
- Attrition Rate
- Average Salary
- Average Age
- Attrition by Department
- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Education
- Attrition by Salary
- Job Satisfaction Analysis

---

## Business Insights

- Sales department experienced the highest attrition.
- Employees with lower salaries had higher turnover.
- Most attrition occurred among employees aged 26–35 years.
- Life Sciences recorded the highest employee count.
